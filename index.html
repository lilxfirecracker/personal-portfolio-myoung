Dancing

I've been dancing since I was 3 years old. I started with ballet and tap. In middle school, I did ballet, tap, jazz, contemporary, lyrical, and hip hop. When I was in the 9th grade, I transitioned into Polynesian Dance. I now dance professionally with the Aloha Dancers.

Skating Life

I have done figure skating since I was 2 1/2 years old. It is my passion and I love every second that I am on the ice. I have multiple showcase figure skating titles including 2017 Gold Rush Showcase Champion, 2017 US National Showcase Junior Dramatic Bronze Medalist, 2017 ISI Open Platinum Free Skate World Champion 2019 Spring Preview Champion, 2019 Crystalline Classic Michelle Van Dyke Award Winner, and 2019 Crystalline Classic Overall 3rd Place.

Here's a link to a video of my program that won the 2017 Gold Rush Showcase championships: Morgan Young 2017 Gold Rush

Modeling & Acting Career

I have done modeling and acting since I was 2 1/2 years old (the same age I started skating!). I am a signed model with Models Inc. Talent Agency and I work out of the San Francisco Bay Area.
